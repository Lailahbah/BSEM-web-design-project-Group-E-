Why SaLone Market

Everything You Need to Trade

Direct Farm Listings

Farmers list produce directly with live pricing — no middlemen cutting into your margin.

📱

Mobile-First Design

Works smoothly on any phone — even on 3G networks across rural Sierra Leone.

Secure Payments
