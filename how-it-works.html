How It Works

Get started in minutes — whether you're a buyer or a seller anywhere in Sierra Leone.

01
📝

Create Your Account

Register as a buyer or seller with your district and contact details in under 2 minutes.

📦
02

List or Browse

Sellers post produce with prices. Buyers browse by category, district, or keyword search.

03
🛒

Place Your Order

Add items to your cart, choose delivery or pickup, and confirm with your mobile number.

04
🚚

Receive & Pay

Goods delivered to your door or collected from the seller's location. Pay on delivery or via mobile money.
