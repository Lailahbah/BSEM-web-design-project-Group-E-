About Us

SaLone Market is a centralized platform designed to support farmers and buyers in Sierra Leone by providing easy access to markets and fresh produce. The platform was created to alleviate the challenges faced by local farmers when trying to sell their harvest, ensuring they get fair prices while reducing food waste. Buyers have a reliable source for organic and fresh produce.
